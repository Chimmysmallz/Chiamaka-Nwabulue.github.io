Inclusive Banking

HerFidelity

Fidelity Bank's women-focused banking proposition — products and experiences built around the realities of female customers and entrepreneurs.

+50% sign-ups · Year 1Led GTM & partnerships
Problem
Women — particularly female-led SMEs — were underserved by one-size-fits-all banking products, limiting access to credit, savings, and growth tooling.
Approach
Discovery with female customers to map needs and aspirations, then shaping a value proposition that paired financial products with enablement and community.
Execution
Owned product positioning, partnerships with women-focused organizations, and the go-to-market — aligning product, marketing, and regional banking teams on rollout.
Results
Drove a 50% increase in customer sign-ups within the first year and expanded the bank's women-centric engagement and partnerships.
Lessons Learned
Designing for a specific segment with empathy beats generic features — and community is a product, not an afterthought.

Customer Churn Analysis

SQLPower BI
Problem Statement
Identify which customers are at risk of leaving and why, before they churn.
Methodology
Analyzed behavioral and transactional patterns to surface leading churn indicators.
Key Insights
Customers holding fewer than 2 products with no activity in 60 days were 3.4× more likely to churn; ~18% of the base flagged at-risk.
Business Recommendations
A targeted re-engagement play on the top at-risk segment was projected to recover ~12% of would-be churners.

Power BI
Problem Statement

Methodology
